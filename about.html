ABOUT

Ruth graduated from University of Maryland in 2013 with a major in Media and Comuunication Studies and a double minor in Spanish and Journalism.
    Upon moving to New York she began working with New York Daily News and received admission to Columbia University Graduate School of Journalism
where she is focusing on CompSci and Journalism. She is now working at Rolling Stone magazine.

She has experience working in PR with Clear Channel Communications' promotional team and MODE PR Showroom.
Ruth has also worked as a writer for Retriever Weekly and Universal Media and in production with FOX News.
Her extensive experience in the media industry has made her an extremely well-rounded multimedia reporter.
